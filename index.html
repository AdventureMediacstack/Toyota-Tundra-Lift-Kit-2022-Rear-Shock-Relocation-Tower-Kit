Product Information

Our 2022 Tundra Rear Shock Relocation Tower Kit is specifically engineered for lifted 2022 Toyota Tundra models equipped with aftermarket suspension systems. When a lift is installed, the factory rear shock mounts often create poor shock angles, leading to binding, compromised ride quality, and reduced wheel articulation.

This relocation kit solves those issues by repositioning the rear shock mounts upward and inward, restoring proper geometry and allowing for the full movement of extended shocks. The result? A smoother, more capable suspension on and off the road.
